Our Full-proof Development Process

Every solution we develop as a software development company undergoes various levels of planning and perfection. Our customer-centric development process includes numerous touchpoints, from understanding client expectations to testing and support.

Understanding Client Expectations

Every service you opt for at Uncanny begins with a 1:1 call with our clients. Our subject-matter experts jump on a call to define your unique project requirements.

Planning and Design

Once we’ve laid the groundwork, it’s time to work on the architectural framework for your solution. Our team lays out a comprehensive design to resolve the pain points.

Test Runs and Debugging

Following a successful phase of planning and design for your business, our experts will test the solution in a simulated environment. We check different aspects of the product.

Final Quality Analysis

Before we deliver the solution, we run a final quality analysis. During this stage, we extensively focus on its scalability and optimization.

Delivery Backed by Guarantee

Finally, it’s time to deliver and implement the solution we’ve designed for your business. Once integrated, our experts will guide you and promise 24/7 support.
